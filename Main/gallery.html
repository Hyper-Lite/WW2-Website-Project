Soviet T-34 Medium Tank

The T-34 tank, widely regarded as one of the most effective and iconic armored vehicles of World War II, played a pivotal role during key battles such as Operation Barbarossa and the Battle of Stalingrad. Its combination of sloped armor, powerful 76.2mm gun, and superior mobility allowed Soviet forces to outmaneuver and outgun German tanks, making it a formidable adversary on the Eastern Front and a symbol of Soviet resilience during the war.
